Origami

Origami is a where you create a figure only using folds. You cannot use glue, tape, or anything that helps the paper hold together. Origami varies in many ways. You can create 3D origami where you create the same piece over and over again, then take those pieces and put it together like a puzzle. You can also create the traditional origami where you only use one piece of paper that is usually square shaped. I really like origami because you can never run out of ideas of what to create. Another reason why I like origami is because it helped me be more precise on folding and cutting, but I would say overall that its just the creative aspect that I like about it. That is just because it makes it really challenging.
